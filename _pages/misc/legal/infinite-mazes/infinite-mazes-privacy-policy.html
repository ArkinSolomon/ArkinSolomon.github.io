Infinite Mazes Privacy Policy

Arkin Solomon, the distributor and copyright holder of “Infinite Mazes” (the “Game”), collects no personally identifiable data, however, the Game uses third-party software from Unity Software Inc. in order to display advertisements, and collect data analytics. The data collected, as well as opt-out information, may be found at Unity Software Inc.’s privacy policy located online at: https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy. Contact Arkin Solomon at arkin@arkinsolomon.net for any questions.
